\outcome{Use partial fractions to integrate functions.}
\outcome{Recognize integrals that are good candidates for the method of partial fractions.}

\tag{partial fractions}
\tag{integration}
\tag{antiderivative}
\tag{indefinite integral}



Find the indefinite integral of $\frac{6x^2+8x-4}{(x-3)(x^2+6x+10)}$ with respect to $x$.
\[
\begin{prompt}
\int \frac{6x^2+8x-4}{(x-3)(x^2+6x+10)}\d x=\answer{2\ln(|x-3|) +2\ln(|x^2+6x+10|) -4\arctan(x+3) +C}\end{prompt}
\]
