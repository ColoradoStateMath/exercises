\outcome{Use partial fractions to integrate functions.}
\outcome{Recognize integrals that are good candidates for the method of partial fractions.}

\tag{partial fractions}
\tag{integration}
\tag{antiderivative}
\tag{indefinite integral}



Find the indefinite integral of $\frac{9x^2+11x+7}{x(x+1)^2}$ with respect to $x$. 
\[
\begin{prompt}
\int \frac{9x^2+11x+7}{x(x+1)^2}\d x=\answer{\frac{5}{x+1}+7\ln(|x|)+2\ln(|x+1|)+C}
\end{prompt}
\]
