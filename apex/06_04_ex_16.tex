\outcome{Use partial fractions to integrate functions.}
\outcome{Recognize integrals that are good candidates for the method of partial fractions.}

\tag{partial fractions}
\tag{integration}
\tag{antiderivative}
\tag{indefinite integral}



Find the indefinite integral of $\frac{x^3}{x^2-x-20}$ with respect to $x$.

\[
\begin{prompt}
\int \frac{x^3}{x^2-x-20}\d x=\answer{\frac{x^2}{2}+x+\frac{125}{9}\ln(|x-5|)+\frac{64}{9}\ln(|x+4|)-\frac{35}{2}+C}
\end{prompt}
\]
