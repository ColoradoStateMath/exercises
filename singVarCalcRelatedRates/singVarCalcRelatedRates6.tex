\begin{exercise}
\outcome{Solve basic related rates word problems.}
\outcome{Identify word problems as related rates problems.}
\outcome{Understand the process of solving related rates problems.}
\tag{related rates}

A baseball diamond is a square 90 ft on a side.  A player runs from first
base to second base at 15 ft/sec.  At what rate is the player's distance
from third base decreasing when she is half way from first to second base?
\begin{prompt}
  \[
  \answer{3 \sqrt{5}}\,\text{ft/s}
  \]
\end{prompt}
\end{exercise}
