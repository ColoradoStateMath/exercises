\begin{exercise}
\outcome{Solve basic related rates word problems.}
\outcome{Identify word problems as related rates problems.}
\outcome{Understand the process of solving related rates problems.}
\tag{related rates}

Sand is poured onto a surface at 15 cm${}^3$/sec, forming a conical
pile whose base diameter is always equal to its altitude.  How fast is
the altitude of the pile increasing when the pile is 3 cm high?
\begin{prompt}
  \[
  \answer{20/(3\pi)}\,\text{cm/s}
  \]
\end{prompt}
\end{exercise}
