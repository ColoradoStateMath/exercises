\tag{antiderivatives}
\tag{integral}
\tag{substitution}
\tag{definite integral}

Evaluate the following definite integral of $\frac{1}{\sqrt{4-x^2}}$ with respect to $x$:

\[
\int_{1}^{\sqrt{3}}\frac{1}{\sqrt{4-x^2}}\d x=\answer{\pi/6}
\]
